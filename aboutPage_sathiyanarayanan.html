Ramya Shastry is a renowned Bharatnatyam dancer born and raised in Bangalore, India. Indian classical dance has been an integral part of her life since a very young age and she incorporates multiple dance elements into her performances.

Ramya trained with eminent gurus such as Vidwan Shri Nagabhushan and Sreedevi Nrityalayain India obtaining formal training in Bharatnatyam and Mayuri studying the art form’s music, choreography, aesthetics and nuances to acquire a deep understanding of both. She grew up immersed in the culture and traditions of Indian dance.

Ramya started performing publicly at a young age of 10. Since then she has gone on to perform at various prestigious avenues and temples around the world and India’s leading cultural institutions such as Sangeet Natak Academy. She has become a star in the Bharatnatyam dance world.

After nearly two decades of learning Bharatnatyam, she has started her dance school where she teaches young budding artists to learn the art form. She has gained an international following and a wealth of knowledgewhich she passes on to her students every day. She is an enthusiastic and engaging teacher bringing liveliness and passion to her classes. Her pure talent along with dedication to her craft is why her students come to her for Bharatnatyam lessons.

Along with dance, she is also a software developer by profession. She is a Java developer with around 10 years of experience. While work and family takes up much of her time, she continues to strive hard and make time for her passion of classical dance.
